Aims & Scope

A business's starting point and the result of all of its efforts are its products. An inventive product design, cutting-edge materials, effective and innovative production techniques, factory management, a responsive and efficient supply chain, and a driven marketing team are all necessary for a product to succeed in the contemporary market. ICMMI-2024 aims to investigate current industrial engineering and production research and development that improves product quality and sustainability.
