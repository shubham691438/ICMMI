About ICMMMI

A business's starting point and the result of all of its efforts are its products. An inventive product design, cutting-edge materials, effective and innovative production techniques, factory management, a responsive and efficient supply chain, and a driven marketing team are all necessary for a product to succeed in the contemporary market. AIMS & SCOPE

Products are the foundation of any business and the outcomes of all of its efforts. cutting-edge materials, innovative production methods, creative product design, factory. A product must have management, a flexible and effective supply chain, and a committed marketing team in order to succeed in the modern marketplace. The Department of Production and Industrial Engineering at NIT Jamshedpur is hosting the first international conference on modern manufacturing and management intelligence (ICMMMI- 2024), which will take place in June 2024 and is open to both national and international academia and business. The purpose of ICMMMI-2025 is to examine current industrial engineering and production research and development that raises the sustainability and quality of products. A wide range of topics, including but not limited to the following, will be covered at this conference: :
